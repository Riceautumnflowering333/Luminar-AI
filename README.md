## 📄 Frequently Asked Questions

**Does the software change my original photos?**
No. The software always saves a copy of your work. Your original file stays untouched in its original folder.

**Can I use this on a laptop with integrated graphics?**
Yes, but performance depends on your processor. If the program runs slow, lower your monitor resolution or close other open applications.

**Where do my finished exports go?**
You can choose the destination folder before you save a photo. By default, the software creates a folder named "Luminar Exports" in your Pictures library.

**Do I need an account to use the features?**
Your copy is a standalone version. You do not need a login to perform basic photo edits.

**Can I batch process photos?**
Yes. Highlight multiple photos in your library, right-click, and select the edit preset. The software will apply the changes to every photo in the group at the same time. This saves time when you come home from a trip with hundreds of new images.
